DIGITAL SCIENTISTS BLOG PAGE
blog page redesign
  • web design
  • illustration

Digital Scientists wanted to turn their existing blog page into a more partitioned editorial center. Thus, the page was renamed to Perspectives with a sub navigation to each of the different spheres. Each section would have a feature article and several tags. The goal of this part of the site was to get people interested in the voice of the company and to subscribe to the newsletter.

Implementation-wise, they wanted the least effort in porting over existing content from older blog posts. The list format of wide image banner and title/subtitle remained, as well as the actual blog post format. As for the splash image for each of the perspectives, I maintained the general aesthetic of illustration applied on the company page, but tried to go for more abstraction and layering for some visual pull.
