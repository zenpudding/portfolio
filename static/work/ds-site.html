DIGITAL SCIENTISTS SITE UPDATES
company and blog page redesign
  • web design
  • illustration
  • photography

After I transitioned to contract work with Digital Scientists, I redesigned several pages on the company site. This effort was focused on painting the company as a hub of insight and expertise, rather than just a technology service provider. One of the tasks was to turn their existing blog page into a more partitioned editorial center. Thus, the page was renamed to Perspectives with a sub navigation to each of the different spheres.

Implementation-wise, they wanted the least effort in porting over existing content from older blog posts. The list format of wide image banner and title/subtitle remained, as well as the actual blog post format. As for the splash image for each of the perspectives, I maintained the general aesthetic of illustration applied on the company page, but tried to go for more abstraction and layering for some visual pull.

Next on the list was the company page. Previously a 'Team' page, this section of the site was dedicated to display aspects of company ideology, culture, members, and community. In the process of establishing a newer look and motif for the site, I repeated thin bounding strokes on most elements. The icon illustrations also set a secondary palette to work with. I had taken the team photos from the previous iteration of this page, so we simply ported them over.
