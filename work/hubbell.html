HPS SELECT - CONNECTOR SELECTOR
new feature to Hubbell catalog app
  • ux design
  • mobile/tablet

Hubbell Power Systems came to us to add a feature to their parts catalog app - an adaptation of a paper configurator wheel that electricians use to replace components on utility poles.

We had to ensure that there wasn't a huge learning curve to go from the analog selectors to the one in the app. Keeping the general flow of steps the same was important in maintaining familiarity.

We kept the analog action of the wheel and reinvented it into a horizontal ticker bar. The results produced from each configuration would link to the existing SKU pages of the greater app. To further smoothen the transition, we included a tutorial that would load on first use.
