I've been a film enthusiast since college, where I had a few classes on history and production. Plenty of practice followed with club activity recordings, fraternity recruitment videos, and participation in Campus Movie Fest. While working as a UX/Visual Designer, I had the opportunity to use some of these skills creating video assets for sites and apps.

An example is the usage of video backgrounds for a header carousel on a page for the Digital Scientists website. Since we were providing a service called "Contextual Solution", ambient video served as the "context" for taglines and apps.

At Digital Scientists, we periodically worked on our in-house app, an online shopping tool called Rately. Many times, it was hard to describe the app to others, in which case pointing to a video helped greatly convey its functionalities short of them actually using the app. We used live footage for tutorials that we posted on the Rately blog, and used screen-only footage for the landing site.
